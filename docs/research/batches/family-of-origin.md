# Framework: Family-of-Origin Influence

> Batch produced for the YouTube topic-gap map (P3). Verified DOIs only.
> A developmental, **non-clinical** lens for the "childhood / parents shaped me"
> demand cluster, via differentiation of self (Bowen tradition, operationalized).
> Never blame-framing, never diagnosis.

## Hard Safety Gate (overrides everything)

- Abuse disclosure / crisis language → route to `crisis-detection` FIRST.
- Never coerce a redemptive narrative on real childhood difficulty
  (see `narrative-identity.md` §Cautions).
- Frame as a present-tense dimension to work with, not deterministic damage.

## Foundational Sources

1. Skowron, E. A., & Friedlander, M. L. (1998). The Differentiation of Self Inventory: Development and initial validation. *Journal of Counseling Psychology, 45*(3), 235–246. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1037/0022-0167.45.3.235 — *Tier A.* Differentiation of self across four facets (emotional reactivity, fusion with others, emotional cutoff, I-position): staying oneself while staying connected. Family-of-origin patterns carry into adult relating but are workable, not destiny.

## Application to 2nd-Brain

### Interview Question Examples (validated framing)

**Korean**
- 가족과 있을 때, 평소의 나와 다르게 반응하게 되는 순간이 있나요?
- 가까움을 지키면서도 ''내 입장''을 말하기 어려운 쪽인가요?

**English**
- Are there moments with family where you react unlike your usual self?
- Do you find it hard to hold your own position while staying close?

### Trait Extraction Cues
- Distinguish the four DSI facets: reactivity (flooding), fusion (losing self),
  cutoff (distancing), I-position (staying oneself). Mirror which one shows up.

### Advisor Guidance Patterns
- Frame family-of-origin patterns as workable now, never as fixed damage.
- Surface the I-position lever (staying oneself while connected).
- Never blame parents or coerce forgiveness/redemption; mirror the user's own move.

## Cautions & Limitations

- Non-clinical, developmental lens only — never frame as a condition to treat.
- Childhood-difficulty content is sensitive; crisis gate is absolute.
- Differentiation is culturally inflected — high family interdependence is not low
  differentiation (cross-check `cross-cultural-east-asian.md`).
